About Me

Eric Jia is the project manager, responsible for delivery and provisioning of the company's North America's sales projects. The projects are various from Ethernet Private Line, MPLS VPN, Wavelength, IP Transit, Content Delivery, Cloud Connect, Colocation.

Prior to joining the company, Eric served as Director of Product and Technology for Infinova where Eric led the market strategy, directed the company's product roadmap, managed product line P&L and oversaw North America marketing programs.

Eric holds a Mater in Science degree from Purdue University and a B.Eng. degree from Nanjing University of AA in China.
